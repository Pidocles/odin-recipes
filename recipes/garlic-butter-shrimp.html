Garlic butter shrimp

Description

Garlic Butter Shrimp: Succulent shrimp cooked in a fragrant blend of garlic and butter, finished with a hint of lemon juice and garnished with fresh parsley. A quick and delicious seafood dish that can be enjoyed as an appetizer or main course.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp, peeled and deveined (1 pound)
  • Butter (4 tablespoons)
  • Garlic, minced (4 cloves)
  • Lemon juice (1 tablespoon)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse the shrimp and pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. In a skillet or frying pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the melted butter and saut√© for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and add the shrimp to the pan.
  5. Season the shrimp with salt and pepper.
  6. Cook the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes per side until they turn pink and opaque.
  7. Drizzle lemon juice over the shrimp and give it a quick toss to coat them evenly.
  8. Remove the pan from the heat.
  9. Sprinkle fresh chopped parsley over the shrimp for garnish.
  10. Serve the garlic butter shrimp immediately as an appetizer or as a main course with rice, pasta, or a side salad.
