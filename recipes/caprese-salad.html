Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese salad is an Italian salad, made of sliced fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and sweet basil, seasoned with salt, and olive oil. It is usually arranged on a plate in restaurant practice. Like pizza Margherita, it features the colours of the Italian flag: green, white, and red.

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes (2-3 medium-sized)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese (1 ball)
  • Fresh basil leaves (a handful)
  • Extra virgin olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Balsamic vinegar (1 tablespoon)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash the tomatoes and cut them into thick slices.
  2. Similarly, cut the fresh mozzarella cheese into slices.
  3. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving plate, alternating them.
  4. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  5. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
